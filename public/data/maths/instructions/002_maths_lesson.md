# Screen 1: What Are Negative Numbers?

Imagine you're playing a game where you can score points or lose points. When you lose points, you go **below zero** - that's where negative numbers live!

Negative numbers are numbers **less than zero**. They're written with a minus sign in front, like **-5** or **-12**.

## The Number Line

Think of numbers as a line that goes in both directions from zero:

# Screen 3: Subtracting Negative Numbers

This is where it gets interesting! Subtracting a negative number is like a **double negative** - two negatives make a positive!

## The Golden Rule

**Subtracting a negative number is the SAME as adding a positive number.**

Think of it like this: If I take away your debt, you actually have MORE money!
