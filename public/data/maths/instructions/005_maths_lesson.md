# Screen 1: What Are Percentages?

**Percentage** means "out of 100". The symbol is **%**.

You see percentages everywhere:
- **Sales:** "50% OFF!"
- **Grades:** "You scored 85%"
- **Statistics:** "60% of people prefer..."
- **Discounts:** "Save 20%"
- **Battery:** "Phone at 75%"

## Understanding "Per Cent"

The word **"percent"** comes from Latin:
- **Per** = "for each"
- **Cent** = "hundred"

So **50%** means **50 out of every 100** or **50 per hundred**.

## Visualizing Percentages

Imagine a grid with 100 squares:

**Key Understanding:**
- 100% = the whole thing = everything
- 50% = half
- 25% = a quarter
- 10% = one tenth
- 1% = one hundredth

## Why Percentages Are Useful

Percentages let us compare things easily:
- **School A:** 45 out of 60 passed
- **School B:** 70 out of 100 passed

Which is better? Hard to tell! But as percentages:
- **School A:** 75% passed
