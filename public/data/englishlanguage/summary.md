## Assessment Structure

### Paper 1: Explorations in Creative Reading and Writing (50%)
**Duration:** 1 hour 45 minutes (AQA/OCR) or 2 hours (Edexcel)  
**Content:**
- Section A: Reading Fiction (20th/21st century for AQA, 19th century for Edexcel)
  - Explicit information retrieval
  - Language analysis
  - Structure analysis
  - Critical evaluation
- Section B: Creative Writing
  - Narrative OR Descriptive writing
  - Inspired by reading section themes

**Total Marks:** 80 (40 for Reading, 40 for Writing)

### Paper 2: Writers' Viewpoints and Perspectives (50%)
**Duration:** 1 hour 45 minutes (AQA/OCR) or 2 hours (Edexcel)  
**Content:**
- Section A: Reading Non-Fiction (one 19th century, one 20th/21st century)
  - Explicit information and inference
  - Summary and synthesis
  - Language analysis
  - Comparison of ideas and perspectives
- Section B: Transactional/Persuasive Writing
  - Articles, letters, speeches, reviews
  - Expressing viewpoints on related themes

**Total Marks:** 80 (40 for Reading, 40 for Writing)

### Spoken Language Endorsement (Reported Separately)
**Not contributing to final grade**
- Pass, Merit, or Distinction
- Formal presentation on chosen topic
- Response to questions and feedback

## Assessment Objectives

### Reading (AO1-AO4)
**AO1:** Identify and interpret explicit and implicit information and ideas; select and synthesize evidence from different texts

**AO2:** Explain, comment on and analyse how writers use language and structure to achieve effects and influence readers, using relevant subject terminology to support their views

**AO3:** Compare writers' ideas and perspectives, as well as how these are conveyed, across two or more texts

**AO4:** Evaluate texts critically and support this with appropriate textual references

### Writing (AO5-AO6)
**AO5:** Communicate clearly, effectively and imaginatively, selecting and adapting tone, style and register for different forms, purposes and audiences. Organise information and ideas, using structural and grammatical features to support coherence and cohesion of texts

**AO6:** Candidates must use a range of vocabulary and sentence structures for clarity, purpose and effect, with accurate spelling and punctuation (20% of total marks)

### Spoken Language (AO7-AO9)
**AO7:** Demonstrate presentation skills in a formal setting

**AO8:** Listen and respond appropriately to spoken language, including to questions and feedback on presentations

**AO9:** Use spoken Standard English effectively in speeches and presentations
