## Free Lessons (1-25) - Foundation Topics

### Number Fundamentals (Lessons 1-8)
1. **BIDMAS - Basic Operations** ✅ *Content Ready*
   - Order of operations, brackets, multiplication, division
2. **Negative Numbers** ✅ *Content Ready*
   - Addition, subtraction, multiplication, division with negatives
3. **Fractions - Addition & Subtraction** ✅ *Content Ready*
   - Common denominators, equivalent fractions, simplifying
4. **Decimals - Basic Operations**
   - Place value, adding, subtracting, multiplying decimals
5. **Finding Percentages**
   - Converting fractions/decimals to percentages
6. **Percentages - Increase & Decrease**
   - Percentage change, multipliers
7. **Ratio - Simplifying**
   - Simplifying ratios, common factors
8. **Ratio - Sharing Amounts**
   - Dividing quantities in given ratios

### Algebra Introduction (Lessons 9-15)
9. **Understanding Algebra - The Basics**
   - Variables, expressions, terms, coefficients
10. **Simplifying Expressions - Like Terms**
    - Collecting like terms, simplifying
11. **Substitution**
    - Evaluating expressions with given values
12. **Expanding Single Brackets - Basics**
    - Distributive property, multiplying out brackets
13. **Solving One-Step Equations**
    - Using inverse operations
14. **Sequences - Finding Terms**
    - Arithmetic sequences, term-to-term rules
15. **Plotting Coordinates**
    - x and y axes, coordinate pairs

### Shape Basics (Lessons 16-22)
16. **Angles - Basic Facts**
    - Acute, obtuse, right angles, angle properties
17. **Angles in Triangles**
    - Triangle angle sum, isosceles triangles
18. **Angles on Parallel Lines**
    - Corresponding, alternate, co-interior angles
19. **Perimeter - Basic Shapes**
    - Rectangles, triangles, composite shapes
20. **Area - Rectangles and Triangles**
    - Area formulas, units
21. **Area - Composite Shapes**
    - Breaking down complex shapes
22. **Circles - Perimeter and Area**
    - Circumference, area of circles, π

### Data & Probability (Lessons 23-25)
23. **Averages - Mean, Median, Mode**
    - Calculating averages from data
24. **Representing Data - Charts**
    - Bar charts, pictograms, pie charts
25. **Basic Probability**
    - Probability scale, simple probability calculations
